1. Can We All Transform Karamchedu in the Most Meaningful Way?

In today's world, education is the most valuable asset an individual can possess. If there is one thing that can truly transform an individual, a family, a community, and our village, it is education.

I firmly believe that education is the key to uplifting and transforming our village, Karamchedu.


2. So Can We Provide Education Like the Best Schools in the Country?

  • We can't expect our village school to match the facilities of the best schools in the country, such as those in major cities, given the obvious limitations. Nor can we ask parents in our village to pay city-level fees to achieve this.
  • As individuals who grew up during the digital era, we have witnessed firsthand how technology can bridge the gap between a person in a remote village and someone in the most developed city. Technology has significantly reduced, if not erased, this disparity. All we need to do is embrace it and empower ourselves with it to reduce the gap between our school and the best schools in our country.

3. How Can We Use Technology to Emulate the Best Schools?

The main motivation behind this idea is that along with digitization, we can use tools like ChatGPT as virtual teachers to support school teachers and help students understand concepts better.

  • Use Projectors/SmartBoards (works Identical to Android tablets but on a bigger screen, commonly used by schools in cities) to teach lessons with videos and pictures.
    • As the saying goes, a picture is worth a thousand words. Imagine watching a movie based on your 10th class non-detail "Hounds of Baskerville." It would be much easier to understand and save a lot of time, allowing students to focus on learning rather than memorizing lessons they don’t fully grasp.
    • Consider explaining the concept of reflection or the behavior of the moon, sun, and earth in our solar system without showing this YouTube video. Visual aids make complex topics clearer and more engaging.
  • Use online teaching platforms and resources like Khan Academy, YouTube videos, and educational images, etc., on lesson-related topics.
    • Empower teachers and students with the best resources for learning, providing access to high-quality educational content and interactive lessons.
  • Use AI tools like ChatGPT
    • To quote an example, you can share your screen and ask ChatGPT to solve/explain any problem/topic and have multiple people speak to it in real time.
    • To explain any topic in the simplest way in Telugu/English.
    • Solve any problem (be it math, physics, English, and so on).
    • Improve communication skills by having a group discussion using that tool to correct students' grammar and communication skills.

4. Can’t We Provide Any of These Facilities at This Moment?

  • Ours is the only school in Karamchedu. The government school has been shut down along with other private schools.
  • Based on our enquiry, schools in neighboring towns charge a minimum of 2.5 times (up to 7 times) the fees compared to ours.
  • Even the school with 2.5 times the fees still uses physical boards and does not have projectors or smart boards in the classrooms.
  • If we were to provide these facilities, we would have to significantly increase the fees, which might not be acceptable or feasible for all the parents in our village.

6. Current State of the School

  • I am happy to inform you that our students performed exceptionally well in the 10th exams. We had a total of 29 10th class students out of an overall 330 students from the 2023-2024 batch:
    • Scores above 500 - 18 students (62%).
    • Scores above 450 - 24 students (82%).
    • First class (360 and above) - 29 students (100%).
  • We have relocated the school from our old location at Chinnavantina Centre to a new building in Hare Rama Ashramam. We had to set up the school infrastructure from scratch as part of this move.
  • I personally spent around 22 lakhs on this new school infrastructure due to this relocation and to provide more facilities. This is another reason why we are currently running on a very tight budget.
